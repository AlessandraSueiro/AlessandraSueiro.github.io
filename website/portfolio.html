Multilingual Literacy Analysis

In my Introduction to Writing, Rhetoric, and Literacy course I conducted an interview with my father to explpore the way language acquisition has evolved throughout his life as a multi-lingual immigrant and the impacts it has on his native language. We both decided that for the purpose of quality and illuminating answers, the interview we be conducted in mostly Brazilian Portuguese and English. I transcribed the interview, translated, and evaluated the way his experiences shape the evolving literacy in his life.
