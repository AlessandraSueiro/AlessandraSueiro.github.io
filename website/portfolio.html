Hunter's Moon

In my Creative Writing course, I wrote two short story pieces to be workshopped and picked one to revise for my final project.

This short story is inspired by Hunter's Moon by Gilbert Vinter, a french horn solo piece I performed with a piano accompanist. It is also inspired by a short story within the novel Once Upon a Broken Heart by Stephanie Garber called The Ballad of the Archer and the Fox.

Creative Writing Collection

In my Introduction to Creative Writing class, we explored writing through many different styles and genres; fiction, non-fiction, and poetry. Through this process, we also workshopped a select few of our writings.

This taught me how to read works through a writer's lens and also recieve feedback and use it to develop my writing even further.

Multilingual Literacy Analysis

In my Introduction to Writing, Rhetoric, and Literacy course I conducted an interview with my father to explpore the way language acquisition has evolved throughout his life as a multi-lingual immigrant and the impacts it has on his native language.

We both decided that for the purpose of quality and illuminating answers, the interview would be conducted in mostly Brazilian Portuguese and English. I transcribed the interview, translated, and evaluated the way his experiences shape the evolving literacy in his life.
